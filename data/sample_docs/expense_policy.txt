Expense Reimbursement Policy

1. Travel:
   - Hotel and flight costs will be reimbursed for official travel.
   - Economy class for flights under 5 hours; business class requires approval.

2. Food:
   - Meals during business travel will be reimbursed up to ₹1500/day.
   - Alcohol is not reimbursable.

3. Local Travel:
   - Use company cab booking app or submit local conveyance bills (with date, distance, purpose).

4. Submission Process:
   - All expenses must be submitted within 14 days of travel.
   - Submit through the SAP portal with scanned receipts.

5. Approvals:
   - Team lead and finance approval required.
   - Amounts > ₹10,000 require director-level approval.
